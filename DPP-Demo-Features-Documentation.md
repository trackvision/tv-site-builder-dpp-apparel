# Digital Product Passport (DPP) Demo - Feature Documentation

## Summary

The Digital Product Passport Demo provides a comprehensive example of how product transparency can be achieved through digital means. It demonstrates:

- Complete supply chain visibility from raw materials to end-of-life
- Compliance documentation with easy access
- Environmental impact tracking (framework in place)
- Circularity metrics for sustainable product management
- Detailed product specifications and sizing
- Full traceability through production and logistics
- Multi-stakeholder information (manufacturers, importers, end-users)

The system serves as an example for future implementations of Digital Product Passports, particularly in the context of EU sustainability regulations and circular economy initiatives.
